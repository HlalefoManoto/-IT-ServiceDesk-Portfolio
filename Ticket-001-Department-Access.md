Ticket-001-Department-Access.md
New department head needs same access as previous manager


Issue Description:
HR has approved a promotion for the employee listed above (the ticket requester). They need to be added to the Management group to access budget reports, approve purchase requests, and manage their team. Promotion is effective immediately.

Troubleshooting performed:
- HR confirmed promotion via official email
- Employee currently has standard department access only
- Manager access approved by department VP

How to resolve this
1
Open Tools → Directory and search for the requester
2
Open their profile
3
In "Add to group...", select "Management" and click "Add"
4
Confirm the group was added
Summary
Role changes require updating Directory group memberships to match new responsibilities. The ticket requester IS the user who needs the access change. Always confirm the request through an authorized channel (HR, direct manager) before granting elevated permissions.

ticket closed.
